### Goal

Connect volunteers with organisations and track volunteer hours.

---

### Users

* Volunteers
* Organisations

---

### Volunteer Can

* Sign up and log in
* View list of opportunities
* View opportunity details
* Apply for an opportunity
* Log volunteer hours
* See approved hours

---

### Organisation Can

* Sign up and log in
* Create and edit opportunities
* View applicants
* Accept or reject applicants
* Approve volunteer hours

---

### Core Features

* Authentication (email + password)
* Opportunity listings
* Applications
* Hour logging
* Hour approval

---

### Data Needed

**Volunteer**

* Name
* Email

**Organisation**

* Name
* Email

**Opportunity**

* Title
* Description

**Application**

* Volunteer
* Opportunity
* Status

**Hours Log**

* Volunteer
* Opportunity
* Date
* Hours
* Approved or not

---
